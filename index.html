About Me

Hello, My Name Is Komarru Jaman. I was born at Bogor City, 10 April 1997. I have graduated from the Department of Informatics Engineering, Bina Insani University in 2020 with a GPA of 3.21. Currently I work in one of the subsidiaries of PT. Telkom Indonesia, which is Antares IoT Solution as an Engineer.

My job description while working at Antares includes: NOC (Network Operation Center) to monitor all projects that have been made, both commercial and trial. Then I was also often assigned to work in the field with a Jobdesc Network Engineer, namely to create and manage a computer network in the project area.

Apart from being a person in the IT world, I also have several hobbies outside of IT, such as music and photography. To see some of my photos, I've created a special gallery to showcase my photos.
